Tourism Guidelines Regulation

Track the progress of environmental policies and initiatives shaping Goa's sustainable future. Transparency in action for a greener tomorrow.

Government Guidelines for Tourists

  • Carry valid ID and required permits.
  • Follow local laws and culture as advised by authorities.
  • Stay out of restricted or protected areas.
  • Use licensed guides, hotels, and transport.
  • Do not damage monuments or public property.

Rules to Protect the Ecosystem

  • Do not litter — always carry your waste back.
  • Avoid plastic bottles, bags, and disposables.
  • Do not disturb wildlife or feed animals.
  • Stay on marked trails to prevent habitat damage.
  • Avoid picking plants, shells, or natural souvenirs.
  • Use eco-friendly transport like cycling or walking.
  • Conserve water and electricity in hotels.
  • Support local eco-friendly businesses and products.
  • Keep noise levels low in forests and beaches.
  • Leave the place as you found it.
